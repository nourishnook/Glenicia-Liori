The Story

This Is What It Looks Like
When Someone Refuses to Stop.

"Be vulnerable. It is truly a superpower."

The Netherlands · 2019–2021

Homeless and pregnant in a foreign country. Moving through shelters in the Netherlands alone, rebuilding from absolutely nothing. I know what the bottom feels like. I built my way out of it once. I am doing it again.

The Climb · 2024–2025

At InvestFest my friends physically pushed me out of my seat when I hesitated. I went forward. I won $25k from Pinky Cole. Then I ranked #1 for seven consecutive weeks in a Forbes competition judged by Daymond John.

The Hard Year · 2025

"From May to November 2025 I had not earned one cent. I applied to 221 jobs. Two hundred and twenty-one."

I took a hostess job way below my level just to buy my ticket. That is not a sob story. That is what it looks like when someone refuses to stop.
